### Risk Register

#### Condition ID: 1  
**Description:** A counterparty must maintain a level of collateral that is a fixed percentage of the mark-to-market (MtM) of the transactions in the netting set.  
**Reference:** doc_0_PI_pages_63-65.txt, Illustration 4: Maintenance Margin Agreement  
**Risks:**  
1. **Risk ID: 1.1**  
   - **Risk Description:** Counterparty fails to maintain the required collateral level, leading to increased credit risk.  
   - **Likelihood:** Medium  
   - **Impact:** High  
   - **Source Reference:** doc_0_PI_pages_63-65.txt, Illustration 4  

2. **Risk ID: 1.2**  
   - **Risk Description:** Market volatility causes the MtM value to fluctuate significantly, making it difficult to maintain the fixed percentage.  
   - **Likelihood:** High  
   - **Impact:** Medium  
   - **Source Reference:** doc_0_PI_pages_63-65.txt, Illustration 4
